About

Spending time at the gym has always been one of my favorite pasttimes and when I was tasked with creating a capstone project utilizing knowledge gained during my three month bootcamp with General Assembly, I leapt at the idea of creating a fitness app.

Arnold is meant to be an app for fitness enthusiasts of all levels. At the moment, the workouts available include premade plans for Yoga, Bodyweight and Boxing exercises. While the workouts themselves are listed for 3-5 minutes, users are encouraged to repeat them 4-5 times.If you are checking out this app, please feel free to send me some feedback on things you like and areas I can improve on.

    • Features

    1. Interval Workouts for one of three categories: Yoga + Boxing + Bodyweight
    2. Log progress in each workout and see how you did for a specific exercise based on date
    3. User Auth
    4. Full CRUD for entries
    5. Relationships between several models

    Ideas for the Future...

    1. Custom Workout Creator
    2. Chart visualization for workouts and progress over time
    3. Drag drop feature to make your own workouts from the workouts available in exercise index
    4. Set a national recommended average for each workout. If user logs an entry with greater repetitions than average highlight that set in green for encouragement.
